Adopt a marine forest

Citizens, organizations and companies are encouraging conservation, understanding and appreciation of local marine environments by adopting a marine forest.

“Adoptive parents” are currently monitoring the conservation status of local marine forests, and organizing activities to raise awareness and appreciation of marine environments. What to adopt a marine forest? Send us an e-mail to marineforests@ualg.pt.

Adoptive parents raising awareness

Plastic Punch, in collaboration with the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and the European Union in Ghana marked the 2022 World Ocean Day with a beach clean-up exercise, calling on citizens to take action to save the oceans from plastic pollution for a sustainable future.
